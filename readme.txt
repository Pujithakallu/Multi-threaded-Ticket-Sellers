ReadMe File
===========

Compile:
	$ make

Execute:
	$ ./main num #where, num is no of customer per queue

Example:
1. To simulate ticket sell with 5 customer arriving at random time for every seller queue
	$ ./main 5 > output_5.txt
2. To simulate ticket sell with 5 customer arriving at random time for every seller queue
	$ ./main 10 > output_5.txt
3. To simulate ticket sell with 5 customer arriving at random time for every seller queue
	$ ./main 15 > output_5.txt
